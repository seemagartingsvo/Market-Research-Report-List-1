<p><strong>The Digital Textile Inks Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Dispersed Inks</li><li>Reactive Inks</li><li>Acid Inks</li><li>Pigment Inks</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The digital textile inks market comprises several types, each suited for specific applications. Dispersed inks are primarily used for dye-sublimation on synthetic fabrics, delivering vibrant colors. Reactive inks bond chemically with fibers, ideal for cotton and other natural textiles, offering excellent wash fastness. Acid inks are specially formulated for protein fibers like silk and nylon, producing vivid hues. Pigment inks, known for their versatility, adhere to a wide range of materials, providing durability and color retention without the need for fixation.</p></p>
